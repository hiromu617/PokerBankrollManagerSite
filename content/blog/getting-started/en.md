## Welcome

Welcome to Poker Bankroll Manager! This guide will walk you through setting up the app and its basic features.

## Recording Sessions

1. Tap the "+" button on the home screen
2. Select the game type (Cash Game or MTT)
3. Enter the stakes and venue
4. Record your results when the session ends

## Managing Your Bankroll

The Bankroll screen lets you track deposits and withdrawals, with charts showing your bankroll trend over time. Multiple currencies are supported.

## Using Analytics

The Analytics screen provides insights including:

- Profit & loss by stakes
- Profit & loss by venue
- Performance over time periods
- Hourly rate calculations

Use these insights to improve your game and make better decisions at the table.
