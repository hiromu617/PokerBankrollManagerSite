# Privacy Policy

Kawai Hiromu (hereinafter referred to as "the Operator") establishes the following Privacy Policy regarding the handling of user information in the application "Poker Bankroll Manager" (hereinafter referred to as "the App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information.

### (1) Information Entered by Users

- Session record data
- Amounts, currencies, stakes, and venue information
- Other data voluntarily entered by users

This information is, in principle, stored on the user's device.

---

### (2) Automatically Collected Information

The App uses the following external services.

### Subscription Management

**RevenueCat**

- Purchase information
- Subscription status
- Device identification information

### In-App Purchases

Apple's In-App Purchase System

- Payment information is not shared with the Operator
- Payments are processed by Apple

### Advertising

Google AdMob

- Advertising identifiers (IDFA, etc.)
- Device information
- Usage data

---

## 2. Purpose of Use

Collected information is used for the following purposes.

- Providing app functionality
- Subscription management
- Advertising optimization
- Usage analysis
- Prevention of unauthorized use

---

## 3. Disclosure to Third Parties

The Operator will not provide user information to third parties, except as required by law.

However, information may be shared with the external service providers listed above to the extent necessary for providing their services.

---

## 4. Data Storage

Data entered by users is, in principle, stored on the user's device.

It is not stored on the Operator's servers.

---

## 5. Minors

Minors must obtain the consent of a parent or guardian before using the App.

---

## 6. Security Measures

The Operator implements reasonable security measures to prevent information leaks.

---

## 7. User Rights

Users can restrict ad tracking or delete data through their device settings.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Policy may be changed as necessary. Changes take effect upon publication within the App or on related pages.
